Hainan

Yalong Bay

Yalong Bay is a crescent bay with a 7-kilometer-long silver-white beach and the sand is quite delicate. The South China Sea is not polluted, the sea is clean and transparent, and there are several different blue colors in the distance. The corals under the water are rich in species, and the corals can be clearly seen. It is suitable for a variety of underwater activities including diving, making the seabed a local one. The core of tourism. The trees on the shore are lush. In winter, the temperature here is 27 degrees Celsius and the water temperature is 20 degrees Celsius. It is a winter resort and leisure resort. Known as "Oriental Hawaii." There are many high-end hotels along the coast, including the international five-star holiday hotel.

Wuzhizhou Island

Wuzhizhou Island is located in Haitang Bay in the north of Sanya City. It is opposite to Nanwan Monkey Island in the north and Yalong Bay in the first bay of Meiyutianxia in the south. Wuzhizhou Island is 2.7 kilometers away from the coastline and has a radius of 1.48 square kilometers. It is irregularly butterfly-shaped, with a length of 1,400 meters from east to west and 1,100 meters from north to south. It is 30 kilometers away from Sanya City and 38 kilometers away from Phoenix Airport. It is close to the expressway from Haikou to Sanya. The location is superior and the transportation is convenient. Wuzhizhou Island is one of the few small islands with fresh water resources and rich vegetation around Hainan Island. There are more than 2,000 kinds of plants and a wide variety.
