Hainan 

Hainan is an island province of China and the nation’s southernmost point. It's known for its tropical climate, beach resorts and forested, mountainous interior. The southern city of Sanya has many beaches that range from 22km-long Sanya Bay to crescent Yalong Bay and its luxury hotels.

Changbai Mountain 

The Changbai Mountains are a major mountain range in Northeast Asia. The mountains extend from the Northeast Chinese provinces of Heilongjiang, Jilin and Liaoning, across the border between China and North Korea, to the North Korean provinces of Ryanggang and Chagang.


Zhangjiajie 

Zhangjiajie, a city in the northwest of China's Hunan province, is home to the famed Wulingyuan Scenic Area.

Hangzhou 

Hangzhou is the southern terminus of the ancient Grand Canal waterway, which originates in Beijing.

Wuzhen 

Wuzhen is a historic scenic town, part of Tongxiang, located in northern Zhejiang Province, China.

Delta Air Lines 

Delta Air Lines, Inc. is a major United States airline, with its headquarters and largest hub at Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport in Atlanta, Georgia.

Cathay Pacific 

Cathay Pacific Airways Limited is the flag carrier of Hong Kong, with its head office and main hub located at Hong Kong International Airport.
